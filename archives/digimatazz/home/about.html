Prajyoti Niketan

Prajyoti Niketan - (Pra means Supreme, Jyoti means Light, Niketan means Centre) ”The Centre of the Supreme Light” is a Vision of Redeeming Love(God) as the Supreme Light for life through daily struggles (Mt.5.48) to achieve fullness / Wholeness (Jn.10.10). It is an International Centre for research, training and service in Wholistic Approach.

Digimatazz2K19

The staff and students of the faculty of Computer Science are organizing a National level Tech Fest entitled 'DIGIMATAZZ 2K19', on the occasion of the Silver Jubilee Year of the inception of our Institution. The Tech Fest has been scheduled, with the intention of showcasing the hidden talents of the students in various aspects of digital technology through their participation in 9 creatively designed competitions.
